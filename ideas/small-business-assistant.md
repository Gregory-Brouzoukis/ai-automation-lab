# Small Business Assistant

This idea is about creating an assistant that helps a small business with simple daily tasks.

## Problem

Small businesses often have many repeated tasks.

They answer messages, collect customer questions, manage reviews, check bookings and organize information.

Many of these tasks can be supported by simple automation.

## Basic idea

The assistant could help the business owner keep customer communication organized.

It could collect messages, create summaries and suggest simple next actions.

## Possible features

1. Collect customer messages
2. Organize common questions
3. Create reply drafts
4. Help with review requests
5. Track booking requests
6. Keep simple notes about customer needs
7. Create daily summaries

## First simple version

The first version could be a document based assistant.

It could take example messages and create organized notes or reply suggestions.

## Future version

A future version could connect with email, website forms, calendars or spreadsheets.

## Why this project matters

This idea is useful because many small businesses need better organization but do not need a complex system.

A simple assistant could make their work easier and save time.
